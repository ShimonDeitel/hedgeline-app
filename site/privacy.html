Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026.

Hedgeline is an offline-first app. All trims you log are stored locally on your device in the app's private storage. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. The app makes no network requests of its own.

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple's StoreKit. We never see or store your payment information.

If you delete the app, your data is deleted with it. There is no account, no server, and no analytics.
